Wang Churan

Introduction

Wang Churan, a mainland Chinese actress and singer, was born in Shanghai on January 21, 1999. She graduated from Shanghai Theatre Academy. In 2017, he starred in his first TV series "Oh My General", thus officially entering the show business. In 2018, she starred as Ling Kong in the sci-fi mystery series "Kun Lun Gui". In 2019, she starred in the urban drama "The Last Goodbye To Mama". In 2020, she will play the role of imperial concubine Zhang Bihan in the ancient palace drama "Held in the Lonely Castle". In 2022, he starred in the ancient food drama "Royal Feast". 2023, starring in the urban drama "Fireworks of My Heart" broadcast.
